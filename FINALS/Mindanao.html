The Hinatuan Enchanted River, also known as the Hinatuan Sacred River, is a deep spring river on the Philippine island of Mindanao. At Barangay Talisay, Hinatuan, Surigao del Sur, it empties into the Philippine Sea and the Pacific Ocean.

Cagayan De Oro (CDO) is known as the Philippines' whitewater rafting capital. Whitewater rafting is the most popular extreme sport in CDO among adrenaline junkies."
Magpupungko Rock Pools is a collection of salt-water pools on rocky flats ideal for swimming and cliff jumping. The Magpupungko Rock Pools are located on Siargao Island's eastern coast, in the Philippines. It's about 40 minutes from General Luna's main tourist hub.
White Island is a must-see destination for tourists seeking white sand beaches, even if only for a short visit. When the weather is nice, the island-sandbar is more popular with its C-shaped island, as it usually changes shape depending on the weather and tide conditions.
